Recent Development

Liwa Water Technology and Solutions (MWTS) has provided more than 100 clarifiers to various large water & wastewater treatment plants. Combined with the vast experience of Manufacturing, Murugappa Water Technology and Solutions (MWTS) has started to offer a wide range of water/wastewater treatment chemicals which are key in enhancing the treatment efficiency of the clarification systems. The product range covers Coagulants, Flocculants, color removal chemicals which are picked according to the nature of the water/ wastewater, tested on the field, and presented to the client as a package treatment solution.
