Flash Mixer

The coagulant shall be added into the flash mixer to destabilize the charge of particles that are present in suspension. After charge destabilization, the particles will come together and form flocs. The chemical addition helps the suspended matter to coagulate and improve the efficiency of suspended matter removal. The suitable coagulants are chosen. The mixing shall be carried out in a flash mixing tank with the help of an agitator.

Features

  • Liwa Water Technology and Solutions (LWTS) Flash mixers are specially designed and fabricated for the process requirement of water and wastewater treatment.
  • The mixer design ensures efficiency, minimum energy consumption, and long life.
  • This equipment blends coagulants and other chemicals with water/wastewater before flocculation.
  • The aggressive agitation results in instantaneous and effective mixing of chemicals.
  • This unit is also useful for general mixing.
