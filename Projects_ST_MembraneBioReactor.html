Membrane Bio Reactor ( MBR )

A combination of activated sludge process and membrane filtration

Key Features

  • Skid mounted systems
  • Easy to install
  • Modifications / upgradation of existing systems are possible
  • Integrated membrane cleaning system
  • Reduces Footprint

Application

  • Small, medium, large sized Sewage treatment plant
  • Industrial ETP like pharma, distilleries, sugar, breweries etc.
  • Revamping or upgradation of exisiting systems.
