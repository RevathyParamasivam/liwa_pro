Aerators

Our High-Speed Floating Aerator is designed to achieve maximum oxygen transfer and minimum energy consumption. This has been accomplished by designing the aerator to be an externally efficient axial flow pump.

Features

  • Rugged Reliable, domestic fabrication at our Chennai factory.
  • Multiple seals with SS 316 cast propeller of anti-fouling two-bladed designs.
  • SS304 shaft provided with special bearings.
  • 3-way protection system safeguarding the bearing from the effluent.
  • Designed to achieve maximum oxygen transfer 1.62 kg o2/hp/hr to 1.71 kg o2/hp/hr.
  • FRP float with integral FRP throat, inlet volutes, and counterweight ballast assembly.
